Chennai Flood Hazard FAQs

What are the common causes of floods in Chennai?

Floods in Chennai are commonly caused by heavy rainfall, overflowing rivers, poor drainage systems, and cyclonic storms.

How can I prepare for a flood?

To prepare for a flood, you should create an emergency kit, develop an evacuation plan, stay informed about weather conditions, and secure your home.
